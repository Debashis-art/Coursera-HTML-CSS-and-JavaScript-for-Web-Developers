Details of the institution

ODM Public School is the flagship school of ODM Educational Group, Odisha's emerging educational group. Over the past decade, ODM has garnered a strong reputation as one of the top CBSE schools of the state and definitely as the best residential school of the state because of its dedication and sheer focus on Academics and service to parents. ODM's motto "Be Good and Make Others Good" originates from its three core values, Love, Care and Respect. The school was started back in 1989 and it recently completed three decades in the domain of education. ODM is dedicated to nurturing each and every child under its banner as it solely believes in the vision that "Each and Every Child can Grow". ODM believes in providing holistic education and grooming children for an all round development. The school is CBSE affiliated and provides Science and Commerce stream education in its senior secondary segment. With a world class campus and an international ambience, the school provides state-of-the-art education standards for the children.

RESIDENTIAL FACILITY

HOSTEL is one of the facilities offered by ODM, as a part of its quality drive for holistic education. It provides separate hostels, both in-campus and off-campus for boys and in-campus for girls. AC Hostel rooms are offered to students for their comfortable stay during their two-years’ study at ODM. All the hostels have attached canteens serving hygienically cooked Indian and Continental food. Hostel life at ODM is provided with all the elements which make it a second home for outstation students.


ODM Boarding Provision has-

  • Complete AC Sharing Accomodations
  • Central Water Heating Systems
  • Spacious Rooms with Bunker Bed Provisions
  • 24x7 complete camera supervision
  • Inside the Campus and Outside the Campus accommodation provision
  • In-House Ambulance and Pharmacy Services
  • Hygienic Food Provision
  • Boarding Managers and Floor Coordinators to take care of the children

Why choose ODM’s Hostel Provision?

  • They are inside/outside (within 50 mtrs) from the campus and are completely operated by the school management ensuring complete safety of the children.
  • End-to-End responsibility of the child, right from Academics to safety and comfort.
  • Get more time for integrated study with CBSE and Coaching Provisions and perform better academically
  • Get ample sports opportunities and co-curricular activities opportunities
  • With no-phone, electronic gadgets and strict regulations on outside visit or exposure to negative elements, the hostels provide a distraction-free environment for the students.
